# What is cloud computing
Cloud computing - Using the internet to provide computer services rather than owning and managing your own servers.

# IaaS vs PaaS vs SaaS
IaaS - You rent infrastrature from a cloud provider.
PaaS - Rent the platform to build apps.
SaaS - Complete software delivered over the internet.

# Cloud vs On-Premise
Cloud - Renting IT services over the internet, You-pay-as-you-go.
On-Premise  - Owning and managing everything , You pay upfront for the hardware.

# CapEx vs OpEx
CapEx - Large upfront spending(Buy and own).
OpEx - Rent and pay as you use.
